ABOUT US

Here's a great story

In 2020, Muffy D. Code started working on the journey of being great. Muffy's journey was to build a gymnasium in the metaverse and in real life. The age of lean was the right fit. Years after multiple clubs were open, the name changed to Lean Fitness as it is today. The company's mission was to provide greatness to all folks, whether you're home or on the road. That has and always will be the #1 goal no matter how much Lean Fitness grows. Lean locations opened all along the metaverse shoreline on Otherside to start. In 2022, with 222 clubs in the metaverse, The Lean announced its expansion into the real world. The passion and focus is strong. Opened 24 hours in the metaverse, opens at 4:00a.m. and closes at 11:00p.m. in real life. These has always been the time for lean. Stay lean don't be mean!
